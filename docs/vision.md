# Product Vision: Bitcoin Sniper
We have a hyopthesis that the price of bitcoin is somewhat periodic, and that dips or minima in the price can be identified and used as a buying opportunity. Our product will look at the daily price of bitcoin and ultimately, automatically execute a buy trade when it detects such a favourable dip. To begin with it will simply observe the price and provide some kind of alert if it thinks it is a good day to buy. This is a personal project, the author is the only target user. The goal is to make money.

## Disclaimer
This is a model trading strategy for the purposes of an educational project. It should not be taken as actual financial advice.
